# Format: Standard Tech / Private-Sector ATS

For software, data, cloud, AI, and most private-sector roles. Goal: pass the
applicant tracking system AND impress a hiring manager in a 20-second scan.

## Resume structure (2 pages max)
1. **Header** — name; positioning tagline under it (e.g., "Staff Software Engineer | Data Platforms & AI"); city/prov; email · phone · site · github. No street address.
2. **Summary** — 3–5 lines. Lead with the identity the role wants, the years of experience, the core stack, and one or two headline proof points (scale, impact). Match the tenure framing in the profile (don't fold older non-relevant years into a narrower claim).
3. **Professional Experience** — reverse-chronological, current roles grouped first. Each entry: official/functional title, real employer, location, month/year–month/year. Bullets in **XYZ form** ("Accomplished X, measured by Y, by doing Z") with the tech inlined. Bold a short lead-in phrase per bullet for scannability. Put the most role-relevant content on the role that actually did it.
4. **Earlier Experience** (optional) — condense pre-relevant roles to one line each (title · org · location · dates) to show full history without burning space.
5. **Technical Skills** — grouped, keyword-dense, with the role's top category **first**. ATS keyword extraction rewards a flat, comprehensive list; don't bury the strongest signals.
6. **Education**, **Selected Personal Projects** (optional), **Publications** (optional — weak signal for pure tech roles; drop to save space).

## Targeting per role family
Re-order and re-emphasize from the same facts — don't fabricate:
- **Data platform / data engineer** → lead skills with Data Engineering (ETL/ELT, warehousing, columnar, event-driven); summary = "data platform engineer".
- **AI / LLM engineer** → lead with AI & LLM Infrastructure (MCP, Claude API, RAG, agentic, Bedrock); lead each role's bullets with the AI work.
- **Platform / infra / SRE** → lead with Cloud & DevOps; emphasize zero-downtime, multi-site, monitoring, reliability.
- **Backend** → lead with Backend Frameworks (Django/FastAPI), PostgreSQL, REST/API design, multi-tenant.
- **Architect / principal** → "Senior/Principal Architect" titles, federated warehouses, multi-site, audit infrastructure.

## ATS hygiene
- One font family ideally; the Awesome-CV template is acceptable for modern ATS but generate the `.txt` companion for legacy parsers and paste-into-form fields.
- Real company names (verifiable, keyword-matchable).
- No tables/columns in the plain-text version; `=` dividers, hyphen bullets, ASCII only.
- Quantify where the profile supports it; never invent metrics.

## Cover letter (1 page)
Plain, confident, specific. Open by naming the role and leading with the
single strongest proof point. 3–4 short paragraphs mapping your real strengths
to the posting's needs. Close with fit + thanks. Avoid generic filler and
comparative jabs at other applicants.
