## Honest framings  ⟨the most important section — how to phrase partial fits⟩
Record the exact hedge to use so generated resumes never overclaim:
- **Kubernetes / Rust** → "(familiar)" — used on small projects, not a core strength; do NOT list parallel to core skills. Frame as breadth / fast-ramp.
- **ITIL** → "ITIL-equivalent change/incident discipline" if you follow an equivalent process but aren't certified (NOT "ITIL certified").
- **Tenure split** → "10+ years in software, the last 6 building cloud platforms specifically" if the early years were a different focus — don't imply the whole career was the narrow thing a posting wants.
- **Title** → headline tagline may be elevated; experience-entry titles stay official, especially for government/regulated employers.
- **A standard you meet indirectly** (e.g. HIPAA/PHI, SOC 2) → claim only if you genuinely handled it; otherwise leave out.
